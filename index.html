Try Goalz on Base

All you need is USDC in your wallet to get started with Goalz. Our application is live on Base and ready to help you set yourself up for a better financial future.

TradFi savings accounts and goal tracking apps have issues:

  • Savings accounts often incur a fees 
  • Hard to manage accounts for each goal 
  • May not support automation 
  • Limited options for currency and yields 
  • Goal setting applications not integrated

Goalz solves these problems using decentralized finance tech:

  • No fees to maintain savings accounts 
  • Can easily make 1 account for each goal
  • Supports automation, programmability
  • Many assets supported, including yield
  • All-in-one: goal tracking and accounts
